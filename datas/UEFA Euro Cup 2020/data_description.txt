All match results from The UEFA Euro Cup 2020 in one CSV file.

stage
: Competition Fase/Stage. Eg: Final, Semi-finals, and etc.

date
: When the match happened

pens
: If the match ends with penaltis or normal time.

pens_home_score
: In case of penaltis, the team home scores.

pens_away_score
: In case of penaltis, the team away scores.

team_name_home
: The team home name.

team_name_away
: The team away name.

team_home_score
: The team home's scores.

team_away_score
: The team away's scores.

possession_home
: Ball possession for the team home.
